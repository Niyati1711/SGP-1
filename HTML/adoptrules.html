REQUIREMENTS:

  • You should be above 21 years of age.
  • We ask for a personal reference (this can be a family member, friend, employer) who can attest to your ability to care for a pet.
  • All the adopters need to meet the dog in-person. We donot arrange online meets.
  • Those adopters who live in appartments need to provide proof that pets are allowed in there appartments.

ALL DOGS AVAILABLE FOR ADOPTION HAVE BEEN:

  • Spayed/neutered
  • Examined by a veterinarian
  • Vaccinated to age limit
  • Microchipped
  • Treated with flea/tick prevention medicine
  • Treated with heartworm prevention medication
  • Suited with a collar

ADOPTION PROCESS

  1. Fill out our Adoption Form and wait for our reply. We will notify you as soon as we have a dog up for adoption.
    (Note:All our dogs will only be up for adoption once they are all vaccinated to there age limit.)
  2. After we get in touch we will schedule an appointment for you to meet our little dog.
  3. After meeting the dog you must confirm to us whether you would like to adopt the dog or not.
  4. Meeting the dog won't guarantee that the adoption is approved.As we take multiple applications we will find the best fit for our little friend.It may take time as it is a timely process.
  5. We would also check whether dogs are allowed in your society or appartments.
