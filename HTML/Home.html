About Us

The Animal Haven is a web-based application wherein the welfare of animals is taken care of.

Each animal requires special care and attention which is fulfilled in the organization. The application allows the user to adopt a pet, volunteer towards the animal and find a shelter for rescued animals .Since this application is developed for a non-profitable organization, the amount donated will be utilized for the welfare of the animals.
