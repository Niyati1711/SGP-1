Food Drives

When the coronavirus pandemic forced everything to close down and everyone to stay at home our little stray friends remained suffered hunger. Many animals died due to hunger as there was no food for them. That's when we got the idea for Food drives across the city so that none of our strays have to stay hungry. It is our mission to save these little animals from malnutrion and provide all of them with food.

*Timings for the food drive : 9:00 pm - 11:00 pm
